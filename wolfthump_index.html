About

The wildly beating heart
of every room they enter

If there were such a distinction, Mike Wofchuck would be Chico's "drummer laureate." Since the Bay Area native came to Chico in 1988, he's provided a wide range of beats for some of the area's most beloved musical acts — the original drummer for The Mother Hips, and a past member of MaMuse alongside Karisha Longaker and Sarah Nutting.

In recent years he's led Wolfthump — an Afro-Brazilian drumline featuring an eclectic collection of local artists and musicians. They are the wildly beating heart of every festival, concert, and community event they show up to.

"There's an idea that your mind isn't the main perceiver of that music — it's more of a feeling. It really hits you in the heart. People look for that. They want a release."
— Mike Wofchuck, Bandleader
